Task 1 : Digital Literacy Infographic  
Overview  
This folder contains the infographic created for Task 1 of the  
Digital Literacy project.  
The goal of this task is to design a simple, visually engaging  
resource that helps students understand the basics of digital  
literacy and its importance in everyday academic life.  
What This Infographic Covers  
The design focuses on three essential areas:  
• What is Digital Literacy  
o Understanding how to use digital tools effectively,  
safely, and responsibly.  
• Useful Digital Tools for Students  
o Tools that improve productivity, learning, research,  
and collaboration.  
• Safe Internet Practices  
o Basic cybersecurity habits such as strong passwords,  
avoiding suspicious links, and protecting personal  
data.  
Design Approach  
• Clean and minimal layout  
• Easy-to-read typography  
• Balanced use of visuals and content  
• Focus on quick understanding (at-a-glance learning)  
Purpose  
This infographic is created to raise awareness among  
students and encourage them to:  
• Use digital platforms responsibly  
• Improve productivity using the right tools  
• Stay safe while navigating the internet
